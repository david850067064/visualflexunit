largestDiffAreaDimensionproperty 
public var largestDiffAreaDimension:int

This figure is derived in the following manner: 1. Look at each continuous area of diff pixels (pixels that don't match precisely in both bitmaps). Contiguous is defined as pixels that are above, below, left or right of each other, and doesn't include pixels that only touch on the corner. 2. Determine the bounding rect that encloses each area. 3. Look at each vertical and horizontal dimension for these rects and select the longest one.
